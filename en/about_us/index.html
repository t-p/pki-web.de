About us

The Company "P & K Industrietechnik GmbH" was established in 2001.

The company combines the performances of "R. Krüger Nachf. & Partner GbR" and of "Ingenieurbüro Pfeiffer" in terms of contract management, the design and engineering of driver’s and operator’s cabins as well as of design-bound components of up to a unit weight of 10 t for hoists and conveyors.

Business operations commenced on January 1st, 2002.

Prior to the establishment of P & K Industrietechnik GmbH, the partnership R. Krüger Nachf. & Partner GbR was in charge of marketing/sales and contract management for driver’s and operator’s cabins while Ingenieurbüro Pfeiffer was in charge of design, beginning in 1999.

Our product range excels in the provision of driver’s and operator’s cabins tailor-made to meet the needs and technical specifications of our customers and their clients, our service excels in responsiveness to customer design specifications, and our experience covers a wide range of equipment and furnishings to meet our customers demands.

The list of references demonstrates the acceptance of our achievements.

In 2002 we extended our product range by including further assemblies for hoists and conveyors as well as electrical cabinets, transformer stations, and lattice booms.

In 2002, the interior design of driver’s cabins was upgraded to include operator’s control panels and seats as well as electrical installations according to client specifications, electrical cabinets in standard size or custom-made, air conditioning and air purification systems. In this field we either cooperate with leading German manufacturers or comply with customer specifications.

We aim at proximity to customers, job-related design, excellent implementation quality, and close cooperation with our customers from as early on as the project development phase.
